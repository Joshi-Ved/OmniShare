1. Platform Usage

Welcome to OmniShare. By using our peer-to-peer peer rental marketplace, you agree to these Terms of Service. OmniShare connects owners ("Hosts") looking to rent out their equipment with individuals ("Renters") looking to lease such equipment.

2. Eligibility & Verification

To use OmniShare, you must be at least 18 years old and capable of forming legally binding contracts. We use DigiLocker API integration to verify identities. Providing false or inaccurate information during our KYC process is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account suspension.

3. Booking, Payments & Escrow

  • All payments are securely processed and held in escrow.
  • Security deposits are authorized on the Renter's card prior to the rental period and released within 24 hours of successful item return.
  • Hosts receive payouts minus the platform commission (12%-18% depending on subscription tier).

4. OmniGuard Insurance

Rentals are covered by our OmniGuard insurance policy, subject to deductibles and limitations based on the plan selected at checkout. Damages caused by gross negligence, theft by the Renter, or water damage are explicitly excluded unless Premium coverage is purchased.

5. Cancellations & Refunds

Cancellations made 48 hours prior to the rental start date receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours are subject to a 50% cancellation fee. Hosts canceling a confirmed booking will receive a penalty to their host rating.

6. Limitation of Liability

OmniShare facilitates transactions between peers but does not hold ownership of any listed items. We are not liable for personal injury, indirect financial loss, or punitive damages arising from the use of rented equipment.
