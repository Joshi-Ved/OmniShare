Privacy Policy

Last updated: Oct 12, 2024

1. Data We Collect

We collect information to provide a smooth, secure peer-to-peer exchange experience. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and phone number.
  • Identity Verification (KYC): Aadhaar and PAN details securely matched via DigiLocker API strictly for verification.
  • Transaction Data: Payment histories, escrow details, and booking logs.

2. Use of Your Data

The information we collect is used to:

  • Facilitate transactions and communication between Hosts and Renters.
  • Process payments via Razorpay and securely manage escrow payouts.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure community safety through our identity vetting process.
  • Provide Customer Support and process insurance claims via OmniGuard.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is only shared with trusted third parties necessary for app functioning: payment processors (Razorpay) and government verification API services (DigiLocker).

4. Security

We use industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) to protect data at rest and in transit. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or storage system is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, modification, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Accounts can be terminated from the User Profile section, which will prompt a 30-day data deletion protocol.
